Chamber Annual Meetup

The Chamber Annual Meetup brings together members from various industries to network, share insights, and collaborate on new initiatives. This event is a highlight of the year, providing opportunities for professional development and community engagement.

Community Service

Our community service projects aim to make a positive impact in the local area. Members participate in activities such as clean-up drives, educational programs, and support for local charities, fostering a spirit of giving and unity.

Annual Stakeholders Meeting

The Annual Stakeholders Meeting is a crucial event where key members and partners review the past year's achievements and set the agenda for the future. This meeting ensures transparency, accountability, and strategic planning for the Chamber's success.
